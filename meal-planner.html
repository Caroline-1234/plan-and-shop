Guide

Everything Plan & Shop can do, in one place

📅 Plan

Build your week's meal plan — Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner for each day.

Generate shopping list — turns your plan into a grouped, deduplicated list instantly
Suggest week — auto-fills the week using favourites (60%) and meals not had recently (40%), weighted by season
Copy last week — loads last week's plan as a starting point to tweak
Day templates — save a typical day and apply it in one click
Day notes — add context to any day, e.g. "Darren working late"
Leftovers badge — meals serving more than your household size are flagged automatically
Rotation warning — flags if you've had the same meal in the last 14 days

🛒 Shop

Your shopping list, automatically built from the plan.

Smart deduplication — combines the same ingredient across multiple meals into one quantity
Aisle grouping — items sorted by supermarket aisle; move any item and it remembers
Tick off items — tap to check off; progress bar tracks your shop
Add extras — add one-off items not in any meal this week
Staples auto-added — your staple items are always included
Edit item names — hover any item and click ✏️ to correct or rename
Print — clean 4-column landscape list, ticked items excluded

📖 Meals

Your recipe library — add, edit, filter and discover meals.

Import recipe — paste any recipe text or describe from memory; Claude extracts all details automatically
Paste ingredients — paste a raw ingredient list and it parses quantities and units
Tags — tag meals freely ("batch cook", "freezer", "Darren's favourite") and filter by tag
Season — mark meals seasonal so Suggest week picks the right ones
Usage tracking — each card shows how many times planned and when last had
Not had recently sort — surfaces forgotten meals that deserve a comeback

⚡ Browse

Compact table view of your full library — great for quick scanning.

Sortable columns — click any header to sort by name, type, time, or usage count
Live search — searches across meal names, ingredients and notes
Usage count — see how many times each meal has been planned
Quick edit — edit button on every row opens the full edit form

🛍 Staples

Items you always buy regardless of the week's meals.

Auto-added — active staples appear in every shopping list
Toggle on/off — switch off for a week without deleting, useful for holidays
Aisle grouping — staples grouped by aisle, matching the shopping list
Smart aisle suggestion — aisle suggested automatically from the item name

⚙️ Settings

Customise Plan & Shop to suit how you cook and shop.

Backup & restore — export all data to a dated file; restores everything including history
Export meal stats — download a ranked report of most used meals and week history
Auto backup — automatically saves a backup every Sunday evening
Metric measurements — auto-converts oz, lb, cups to g and ml on import
Household size — set how many people you cook for, used for leftovers flagging
Custom aisles & categories — add your own to match your store and cooking style

Tips for getting the most out of Plan & Shop

Build your library first — aim for at least 20–30 meals across all three types before relying on Suggest week
Use Import recipe — fastest way to add meals; paste from any website or just describe it in plain English
Tag generously — tags like "batch cook", "freezer friendly", "quick prep" make filtering powerful over time
Set up your staples first — add your weekly non-negotiables so they're always on the list without thinking
Plan on Fridays — the week runs Friday to Thursday. Hit Suggest week, tweak a couple of meals, generate the list. Under 5 minutes
Back up regularly — Settings → Export backup after adding a batch of new meals. Takes 2 seconds
Use the feedback tracker — open the feedback file every Thursday evening to capture what's working and what to improve
